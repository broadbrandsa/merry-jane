# Project Overview

## What this is

The **Merry-Jane Proposal Site** is a standalone presentation website that packages two strategic deliverables into a single, navigable web experience:

1. **Digital Audit & Marketing Strategy** (April 2026)
2. **Influencer, PPC & Social Media Marketing Proposal** (April 2026)

Both documents were prepared for Merry-Jane — South Africa's premier cannabis collective — and together they describe a compliant, strategic approach to building digital authority, member growth, and brand visibility under the restrictive advertising environment defined by the Cannabis for Private Purposes Act (CPPA) 2024 and SAHPRA regulations.

## Why a site and not a document

- Clients consume strategy differently online: scroll, scan, share.
- A web surface lets the strategy be updated quarterly (as the regulatory environment requires) without resending PDFs.
- A presentation site doubles as a showcase of what a compliant, editorial, SEO-ready Merry-Jane digital presence could look like — the medium becomes the pitch.

## Target audience

- **Primary**: Merry-Jane leadership and marketing decision-makers.
- **Secondary**: Legal advisors reviewing compliance framing, future agency partners, and internal team onboarding.

This is not a consumer-facing site. It is a stakeholder deliverable.
