# Assumptions

The strategy captured in this site is **directional, not operational**. The following assumptions underpin every recommendation — if any change, the plan should be revisited.

## 1. No internal data access

- No Google Analytics, Search Console, Meta Insights, or CRM data was available at the time of writing.
- All benchmarks (e.g. 0.17% Facebook engagement, 17,000+ members, 15+ collection points) are drawn from public sources and brand statements.
- Member acquisition funnels, CPL, LTV, and churn are **not** modelled in this document.

**Implication**: Once Merry-Jane shares analytics and CRM access, the KPI targets in `/content/kpis` should be recalibrated against actual baselines.

## 2. Directional strategy only

- The roadmap is a 90-day directional framework, not a work-breakdown structure.
- Creative execution (copy, art direction, shot lists, motion) is intentionally out of scope here.
- Budget ranges in the PPC section are starting points, not committed line items.

**Implication**: This site supports a decision. Once approved, a delivery SOW and production calendar must be built separately.

## 3. Regulatory snapshot as of April 2026

- All legal framing reflects the regulatory environment as of **April 2026**.
- Key legislation referenced: CPPA 2024, Medicines and Related Substances Act, SAHPRA guidelines, POPIA, ASA Code, Consumer Protection Act.
- The Hemp and Cannabis Commercialisation Policy is expected before Cabinet mid-2026; the Overarching Cannabis Bill is targeted for Parliament by mid-2027.

**Implication**: All recommendations must be **reviewed by a SAHPRA-aware attorney** before activation — especially influencer briefs, YouTube ad creative, and programmatic DSP content. A quarterly compliance review should be scheduled in perpetuity.

## 4. Platform policy snapshot

Platform advertising policies (Google, Meta, TikTok, X, LinkedIn) reflect published policy as of April 2026. These change frequently. The "BLOCKED / OPEN / LIMITED" status grid must be revalidated before any paid spend commitment.

## 5. Requires validation post-approval

Before any campaign activation, the following must happen:

1. Legal review of every channel strategy and creative template.
2. Technical SEO audit of `merry-jane.com` by a developer familiar with SPA + SSR.
3. Baseline analytics stood up (GA4, Search Console, social native insights, WhatsApp Business metrics).
4. POPIA consent flow audited across email, WhatsApp, and CRM.
5. Influencer brief template finalised with signed-off compliance language.

Without these, the 90-day roadmap cannot start.

## 6. No existing asset audit

Brand assets ("Faces of Merry-Jane" series, cultivation photography, clinician bios, patient testimonials) are referenced generically. No physical inventory of creative assets was conducted.

**Implication**: Asset production may be required ahead of content launch — budget and timeline to be confirmed.
